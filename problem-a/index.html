A fictional character that I admire is Mulan

Description

Mulan is a Disney princess that is brave and self-reliant. Mulan is specifically a warrior, unlike a lot of other princesses. Wanting to protect her country and family, she makes the decision to disguise herself as a man to take her father's place in the army. Mulan emphasizes the importance in standing up for what you believe is right, family, and being proud and embracing the culture/background you came from.

Why I admire Mulan

  • Asian representation on TV
  • Seeing a princess with the same cultrual background as me was empowering
  • Strong and resilient
